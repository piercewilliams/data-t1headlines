### 2026-04-03 — ad-hoc analysis: Platform Format Guide evidence assessment

**Context:** Sara Voluone's draft SmartNews/Apple News Platform Format Guide reviewed.
Full re-analysis run against 4,174 Apple News articles, 38,213 SmartNews articles, 1,783 notifications.
Ten analyses executed. Results delivered as .docx report for Sarah's use.

**Signals observed:**
- `what_to_know` significantly underperforms on SmartNews (p=0.046, r=−0.135). Guide recommends it. → **High-value contradiction finding — added to Confirmed Interesting**
- Questions hurt on BOTH platforms (AN: p<0.001 r=−0.265; SN: p<0.001 r=−0.091). → **Universal avoidance rule — high-value**
- Notification formula effects are 2–5× larger than views formula effects. → **Confirms notifications as the highest-signal channel**
- Apple editors over-select questions for featured (1.67×); algorithm penalizes them. → **Novel tension — confirmed interesting**
- SmartNews 80–99 char optimal bin confirmed; 100-char threshold has no statistical basis (p=0.915). → **Refines guide guidance — confirmed useful**
- Apple News curve rises through 110–119 chars; guide's 110-char ceiling is conservative. → **Refines guide guidance**
- Service journalism does NOT outperform hard news on SmartNews (p=0.74). Guide claimed it does. → **Contradiction of guide claim — confirmed interesting**
- Analysis 3 (SmartNews formula): multiple comparisons family = 7 tests, Bonferroni α/7 = 0.0071. WTK (p=0.046) does not survive Bonferroni correction. Should be reported as "directional" not "significant" in the strictest sense. → **Rigor note: report Bonferroni threshold when n of tests ≥5**
