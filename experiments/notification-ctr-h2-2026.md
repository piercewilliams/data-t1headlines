The main site's notification findings (possessive 1.86×, exclusive 2.49×) are based on
Jan–Feb 2026 only (n=351). The Guthrie serial story cluster drove the top of the CTR
distribution in that period.

This experiment validates whether the overall CTR baseline is stable once H2 2026 data
arrives. A significant shift would suggest seasonal patterns or that the Guthrie effect
inflated Jan–Feb baselines. Run when H2 notifications data is available from Tarrow.
