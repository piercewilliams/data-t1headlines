**Exhaustive analysis run (~20 hypothesis classes):** headline length × featuring, active time × featuring, brand-level featuring rates, notification time-of-day, notification headline length, channel-level CTR, SmartNews category H1/H2 trends, saves × featuring, ANP section × formula interaction, ANP publication-level featuring, SmartNews formula performance, cross-platform formula matrix, notification word-level signals (10 tests, BH-FDR corrected), ANP formula × topic combinations.

**Five additions to the site (8 → 13 findings):**

1. **MSN Formula Divergence** — direct declarative headlines 4.84× better than any structured formula on T1 news MSN (p=2.9e-13). Questions/here's/explainers all significantly below baseline. Opposite of Apple News playbook.

2. **Formula × Topic Interaction** — "here's" and question format lift Apple News featuring ONLY on weather content: `heres×weather` 70.6% (n=102), `question×weather` 56.7% (n=67). For all other topics, formula barely predicts featuring (11–18%, near baseline). Topic is the primary driver; formula is a secondary signal within weather/emergency coverage only.

3. **SmartNews Cross-Platform Formula Trap** — question (0.423 rank, p=3.4e-6, n=918) and "what to know" (0.371, p=3.0e-6, n=213) actively hurt SmartNews performance. "Here's" (0.543, p=0.038) is the only formula above baseline on both platforms. Number-lead directionally above SN baseline too.

4. **Notification outcome language** — crime/death outcome words ("dead/died/killed/arrested/charged/sentenced") 1.26× CTR (p_adj=0.0015, n=55) — strongest notification signal post-FDR, ahead of attribution language (1.18×, p_adj=0.020). All other signals (questions, urgency, geo-anchor, superlatives, money amounts, opinion flags) not significant after FDR correction.

5. **Notification send-time** — Evening (18-20h): 0.0155 CTR vs Morning (9-11am): 0.0131. KW across time bins H=27.66, p=4.2e-5. Morning is the most-used window but worst-performing. Directional (evening n=171, night n=37).

**Existing findings extended:** Finding 5 (sports universal underperformance — extended with MSN text, MSN video completion rate, notification CTR data). Finding 4 (notification CTR declining 29% over 9 months Jun 2025→Mar 2026; monthly trend chart added).

**Key analytical notes:**
- Apple News 2026 "featured view lift" appears reversed (featured median < non-featured) due to composition: Us Weekly celebrity articles dominate the non-featured high-view pool. Within-topic, featuring still helps (weather: 2.92× lift). Not a sign that featuring stopped working.
- "What to know" 0% featuring in 2026 stable (vs 54% in 2025) — formula applied to celebrity profiles in 2026 vs weather/emergency in 2025. Content type is the driver, not formula.
- Notification channel-level CTR differences: KW p=0.051 — marginally not significant; not wired.
- SmartNews U.S. category dramatic H2 2025 decline (0.972→0.777 rank, p=7.1e-53) — directional/historical, not wired.
- Brand-level Apple News featuring variance (Charlotte Observer 88%, Modesto Bee 8%) — highly significant but not headline-actionable.
